[ HOW WE THINK ]

01

Minimal by intention, not by accident

Every UI element in TaskFlow is there because it earns its place. We remove features rather than add them by default. The space around your tasks is not empty — it's thinking room.

02

Speed is a feature

A task manager that loads slow is already failing you. TaskFlow is pure HTML, CSS, and lightweight JS. No frameworks, no multi-second hydration, no spinners where there shouldn't be any.

03

Your data belongs to you

No analytics scripts, no advertising SDKs, no selling your usage patterns. JWT auth keeps sessions secure. Your notes and tasks live on servers you point to, not ours.

04

Context next to your work

Notes and tasks shouldn't live in separate apps. The reason you're building something matters as much as the task itself. TaskFlow keeps them connected without making it a project management suite.
